Our Story



Havana Restaurant is a family-owned and operated restaurant that serves authentic Indian cuisine in a cozy and elegant setting. We are passionate about sharing the rich and diverse flavors of India with our guests, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes. Whether you are looking for a spicy curry, a fragrant biryani, or a sweet dessert, we have something to satisfy your taste buds. Our menu also features a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options to cater to different dietary.

At Havana Restaurant, we believe that food is not just a necessity, but an experience. That’s why we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can enjoy your meal with your family and friends. Our friendly and attentive staff will make you feel at home and ensure that you have a memorable dining experience. We also offer catering services for special occasions, such as weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, and corporate events.
