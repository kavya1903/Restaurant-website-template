About Us

Welcome to our restaurant! We pride ourselves on serving delicious meals made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Our experienced chefs are passionate about creating unique flavors and culinary experiences for our customers.

Havana Restaurant is a family-owned and operated restaurant that serves authentic Indian cuisine in a cozy and elegant setting. We are passionate about sharing the rich and diverse flavors of India with our guests, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes.

Popular Dishes

Aloo Tikki

Crispy potato patties served with chutney and tamarind sauce.

Masala Dosa

Dosa filled with a spicy potato filling, served with sambar and coconut chutney.

Palak Paneer

Cottage cheese cubes cooked in spinach and cream sauce, served with basmati rice and naan.

Chicken Biriyani

Basmati rice cooked with chicken, spices, and herbs, served with raita and papadum.

Chicken Tikka

Tender chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, grilled in clay oven, served with mint and tamarind chutney.

Gulab Jamun

Deep-fried milk based balls soaked in sugar syrup.
